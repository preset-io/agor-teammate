## Root Cause

`superset/models/core.py:707` — inner helper `column_needs_conversion` in `Database.post_process_df` used `df_series[0]` (label-based fallback). When the DataFrame has been filtered or reindexed (index doesn't contain label `0`), pandas raises `FutureWarning` and will `KeyError` in future pandas.
