These keys hit `QueryObject._rename_deprecated_fields()` on every chart render, which logs a warning. The `form_data.get("timeseries_limit_metric")` reads elsewhere in `viz.py` (lines 383, 993, 1421, 2648) were kept as-is for backward compat with saved chart configs.

- `"timeseries_limit": limit` → `"series_limit": limit`
